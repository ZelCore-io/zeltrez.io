Multi-asset Encrypted Wallet

ZelTreZ keeps your assets safe. With support for 35+ coins including Zelcash (ZEL), Bitcoin (BTC), Litecoin (LTC), Ethereum (ETH), Zcash (ZEC), Tether (USDT) and many more.

Support for Multiple Accounts

Our login system allows you have multiple encrypted wallets and accounts on the same computer.

Light Node or Full Node. The Choice is Yours.

Zeltrez gives you the power to choose if you want speed or advanced features. All wallets are available as light nodes, with the option to download the Full Node should you required the advanced features like shielded addresses, recovering existing wallets, and much more.

Built-in exchanges

Our users can utilise built-in ShapeShift and Changelly exchange. Swap cryptocurrencies directly inside ZelTreZ platform!
